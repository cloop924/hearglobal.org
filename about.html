We give the world a voice.

HearGlobal was founded on a simple premise: Language is meant to be heard, not just seen.

Our Story

Founded in Albuquerque, NM, HearGlobal LLC started as a small team of linguistics enthusiasts and software engineers. We noticed that existing language apps treated learning like a game of matching text to images.

We took a different approach. By focusing on high-fidelity audio, natural speech patterns, and listening comprehension, we build tools that prepare users for actual conversations, not just exams.

Our Philosophy

We believe in tools that get out of the way. Our mobile applications are designed with strict minimalism—removing gamification clutter to focus purely on the transfer of knowledge. We are committed to accessibility and privacy, ensuring our tools serve everyone, everywhere.
