🎭 Lore

The game includes a light narrative flavor through a mischievous carnival theme, and recurring system expansions that introduce new rule variations over time. Beneath the energetic surface of this "Carnival of the Mind" lies a highly structured mental exercise. The barker at the booth tries to trip you up with misleading text—will your focus shatter under the flashing lights, or can you remain perfectly unbothered?
