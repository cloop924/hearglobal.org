TapTap Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

HearGlobal ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding data collection and usage specifically for TapGame.

1. Data We DO NOT Collect

To protect player privacy, we DO NOT collect, store, or transmit to any external server:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII)
  • Account Passwords
  • Specific Location Data
  • Persistent Cross-App Identifiers

2. Data We Process Locally

All player statistics, profile selections, coin balances, unlocked levels, and progression data are stored exclusively on your device within local persistent storage (e.g., UserDefaults or equivalent secure domains).

TapGame requests local access to your device's Photo Library and Camera solely to enable you to set a custom profile picture. These images are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted to, stored on, or shared with our servers or any third parties.

Deleting the App or clearing its data will permanently erase your progression.

3. Third-Party Services

For in-app purchases or subscription processing, we utilize standard Google Play and Apple App Store secure billing services. These entities govern their respective transactions.

4. Analytics

We may utilize anonymized crashing and performance data to enhance stability, aggregated without attaching external personally identifiable telemetry.

5. Children's Privacy

TapGame does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 online. As it operates offline via local storage, children's data remains on their device.

6. Contact

For inquiries, please reach out to us at: contact@hearglobal.org
