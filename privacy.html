Overview

HearGlobal LLC develops mobile applications focused on language learning and communication. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website or contact us.

Information we receive

  • Contact information: If you email us, we receive your email address and the content of your message.
  • Website usage: This website is a static site and does not require accounts.

How we use information

  • To respond to support requests and business inquiries.
  • To maintain site operations and communicate with you when you contact us.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share information only when necessary to respond to you, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Data retention

We retain emails and correspondence for as long as necessary to respond and maintain business records.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at contact@hearglobal.org.
